Welcome to Version 2!

Leeds Art Map was an independently run crowd-curated free platform for sharing and finding arts events in Leeds.
The site is sadly no longer updated, but should still help you locate some of the many many galleries the city has to offer.
If you are interested in taking ownership of this service, drop us an email
Thank you for your support.
